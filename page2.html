Food and Drink

The Ivy Spinningfields

A stylish restaurant in Manchester opened on November 27th, 2019, after its success in London being famous with celebrities for just over 100 years. This branch has 4 floors with a main diner, private dining room, a cocktail bar, Asian cuisine, DJ set and a roof garden. It is one of the biggest restaurants in the UK. It is open 7 days a week, from 5pm weekdays and 12pm weekends: often open till midnight.

The Jane Eyre

Located on Chorlton's Beech Road – well known cocktail bar and restaurant. Originally was a neighbourhood bar, it has made a name for itself with its Sunday roasts and small bites. Situated on the former site of The Parlour it boasts a classy design, renovated bar and an outside terrace which is always popular in the summer.

The Black Friar

The Salford bar has recently been restored into a modern-day restaurant and pub, it still has its original features and a delightful menu. Great for all year round with its large indoor bar and gorgeous beer garden and outdoor bar. The head chef is former 20 Stories chef, Ben Chaplin. Delicious dishes range from foraged mushrooms on toast to Cumbrian shredded lamb hot pot.

Zizzi Manchester Piccadilly

Is a modern restaurant in the city centre that serves a wide range of food including pizza, chilli, a variety of pasta dishes as well as starters such as garlic bread, meatballs etc. The place has a relaxing atmosphere with not too much lighting and has good background music. It is a good restaurant worth going to.
