Points Of Interest

Manchester Museum

Established in 1867, this is a well-known museum in Manchester that is home to 4.5 million historic pieces including life size dinosaur skeleton replicas, taxidermy of many famous animals and species, as well as the actual Golden Mummies of Egypt.

Science and Industry Museum

The Science and Industry Museum has been around since 15th September 1983, housing different areas of technology including transportation, power, and communication & computers. They have working steam train engines as well as models of aeroplanes that are no longer in operation.

Etihad Stadium

The home of Manchester City Football Club, it is well appreciated by its fans; and can hold a staggering number of 53,400 attendees. They have won multiple leagues such as the FA Cup, Premier League and the UEFA Champions League. They have also won two trebles over the years, one in 2009 in Barcelona and the other in 2023 in Manchester. It is well worth the visit if you’re in Manchester.

Belle Vue Speedway

Is a stadium in Gorton, Manchester for the Belle Vue Aces of British Speedway; is a form of motorbike racing where there is only one gear and no brakes. The Belle Vue Aces are a world-famous club that have won the British league title many times over. They have world class riders in their team and have world class facilities. If motor sport is for you, get yourself down there.
