Shops

The Trafford Centre

Established on 10th September 1998, this shopping centre has 185,000 square metres of retail space and is one of the biggest shopping centres in the UK. Its largest current stores include Selfridges, Marks and Spencer & M&S Foodhall, and JD once its upcoming expansion is complete. It has 200 shops in total. The centre has a holiday type theme while also being professional, with several palm trees throughout the walkways as well as brass banisters and various historic statues. Popular stores here are Superdry, Footlocker, JD, River Island, The Apple Store and more.

Selfridges

Selfridges is a popular designer outlet that sell the likes of Lacoste, Hugo Boss, Burberry among many others. They have proven to be successful in tourist destinations such as Manchester. It also sells household items such as kitchenware, home furniture and toiletries.
